# Meaning Behind the Name: Lumark

Lumark was chosen as the "All-Rounder" winner for our document intelligence platform because it perfectly encapsulates our mission through three core pillars:

## 1. The "Lume" (Light)
Retains the idea of **"shedding light"** on complex data. Lumark illuminates the hidden insights within your documents, making the opaque transparent and understandable.

## 2. The "Ark" (Structure/Archive)
Suggests a **safe place** for documents and a **strong foundational structure**. Like a legendary vessel, Lumark provides a secure and organized environment for your most important information.

## 3. The "Mark" (Precision)
Implies that the AI **"hits the mark"** with its analysis. It also refers to the ability to literally **"markup"** or highlight key information in the PDF, ensuring precision in every insight generated.

---
**Lumark: Illuminate. Secure. Precise.**
